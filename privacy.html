Privacy Policy

Quran Verses  ·  Effective Date: January 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Quran Verses collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our digital content and services. We are committed to your privacy and handle all data responsibly.

1. Who We Are

Quran Verses is a content channel that produces and distributes Islamic educational videos featuring Quranic recitations with multilingual text overlays. We operate on YouTube, TikTok, Snapchat, and other social media platforms.

2. Information We Collect

Quran Verses does not operate a standalone app or website that collects personal data directly. However, in connection with our services, certain data may be handled:

  • Platform Analytics: We access aggregated, anonymized analytics provided by YouTube, TikTok, and Snapchat (views, watch time, demographic ranges). We do not receive personally identifiable information from these platforms.
  • Donation Information: If you donate via PayPal, your payment information is handled entirely by PayPal and is subject to their privacy policy. We only receive confirmation of donation amount.
  • Contact Information: If you contact us voluntarily (via DM or email), we may retain your message to respond to your inquiry.

3. How We Use Information

Any information we access is used solely to:

  • Understand our audience and improve content quality.
  • Respond to user inquiries and feedback.
  • Ensure compliance with platform policies.
  • Process and acknowledge voluntary donations.

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Platforms

Our content is distributed through third-party platforms. When you watch or interact with our content on these platforms, their privacy policies apply:

  • YouTube / Google: policies.google.com/privacy
  • TikTok: tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Snapchat: snap.com/privacy/privacy-policy
  • PayPal: paypal.com privacy policy

We encourage you to review these policies, as we have no control over how these platforms collect or use your data.

5. TikTok API Usage

Quran Verses uses the TikTok API to programmatically publish video content to our official TikTok account. Through this integration:

  • We access only our own TikTok account credentials and publishing permissions.
  • We do not collect, store, or process any TikTok user data.
  • We do not access any other users' profile information, content, or private data.
  • API access is used solely for automated video upload and publishing operations.

6. Cookies and Tracking

This website (quranverse-21.github.io) does not use cookies or any tracking technologies. It is a static informational page hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect limited technical data (such as IP addresses) as part of their standard hosting service; please refer to GitHub's privacy policy for details.

7. Children's Privacy

Our content is suitable for all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it promptly.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect any information we handle. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We do not store sensitive personal data on our systems.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please contact us through our official platform channels.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of our services following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us through our official social media accounts on YouTube, TikTok, or Snapchat under the name "Quran Verses."
